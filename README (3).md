# ❓ Customer FAQ — Common Account & Access Questions

*This FAQ is designed for customer-facing use. Agents can share individual answers directly in chat or email.*

---

## Account Access

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**  
A: Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and enter your registered email address. You'll receive a reset link within 2–3 minutes. If you don't see it, check your spam or junk folder. If it still hasn't arrived after 5 minutes, contact support and we'll resend it.

---

**Q: My account is locked. What do I do?**  
A: Accounts lock automatically after several failed login attempts as a security measure. Contact our support team and we'll unlock it for you after verifying your identity. This usually takes less than 5 minutes.

---

**Q: I'm not receiving emails from your system. What's wrong?**  
A: First, check your spam or junk folder — our emails sometimes land there. If you don't find them, make sure the email address on your account is correct by contacting support. You can also add our domain to your safe senders list to prevent this in future.

---

**Q: How do I update my email address or contact information?**  
A: Contact our support team directly for any account information changes. For security reasons, we verify your identity before making updates to ensure no one else can change your details.

---

## Technical Issues

**Q: I can't connect to the system. Where do I start?**  
A: Start with the basics: check your internet connection, restart your browser, and try clearing your cache. If you're using a VPN, make sure it's connected. If none of that works, contact support and we'll walk you through it step by step.

---

**Q: The application keeps crashing. What should I do?**  
A: Try these steps in order: (1) Refresh the page or restart the app, (2) Clear your browser cache and cookies, (3) Try a different browser, (4) Restart your computer. If the issue continues, contact support with details about what you were doing when it crashed.

---

**Q: I'm getting an error message. What does it mean?**  
A: Error messages vary — the most helpful thing you can do is write down or screenshot the exact message and share it with support. That helps us diagnose the issue much faster.

---

## Billing & Account Management

**Q: I was charged incorrectly. How do I dispute a charge?**  
A: Contact our support team with your account details and the charge in question. We'll review it and get back to you within 1–2 business days. For disputes over a certain amount, we may escalate to our billing team for a faster resolution.

---

**Q: How do I cancel or change my subscription/service?**  
A: Contact support directly and we'll walk you through the options. We want to make sure you have all the information before making any changes to your account.

---

**Q: Where can I find my billing history?**  
A: Log into your account and navigate to the Billing or Account section. If you can't find it or need a specific invoice, contact support and we'll send it to you directly.
