# ✉️ Project 4 — Customer Service Email & Script Templates

A collection of professional response templates for the most common customer service scenarios. Every template is designed to be warm, clear, and actionable — not robotic.

These reflect the communication style I applied consistently to maintain a 96–100% QA score across 4.5 years of remote support.

---

## Templates Included

| File | Scenarios Covered |
|------|-------------------|
| [`email-templates.md`](./email-templates.md) | Written email responses for common situations |
| [`call-scripts.md`](./call-scripts.md) | Phone script guides for handling difficult calls |

---

## Design Principles Behind These Templates

1. **Lead with empathy** — acknowledge before you explain
2. **Be specific** — vague responses make customers call back
3. **Give a clear next step** — never end without telling them what happens next
4. **Keep it human** — no one wants to feel like they're talking to a bot
5. **Own the problem** — even if it wasn't your fault, the customer's frustration is real
